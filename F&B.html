Udders Ice-Cream

Udders Ice-Cream

Established: 2007
Type: Master Franchise (Maharashtra, Delhi)
Franchise Fee: USD$100,000
Set Up Cost: USD$80,000 Per Unit
ROI: 18 Months

The brand has a diverse and inventive flavor range, particularly the integration of Daring Local Flavors reflecting Asian flavors, and a popular, distinct Alcoholic Range appealing to the adult market.

Quality is ensured through the use of fresh, natural ingredients and a commitment to delivering a dense, high-butterfat ice cream that guarantees a satisfyingly creamy texture.

The brand has an inclusive range of Dairy-Free and Vegan Options, alongside Lower-Sugar Selections.

EAGLE BOY
Pizzeria & Cafe

Eagle Boys Pizzeria & Cafe

Established: 1992
Type: Master Franchise (Tamil Nadu, Delhi)
Franchise Fee: USD$80,000
Set Up Cost: USD$60,000 Per Unit
ROI: 15 Months

The pizza chain was founded in 1992 and has evolved to position itself as a healthy and delicious pizza chain.

All the ingredients are freshly prepared and the dough of the pizzas are prepared fresh every morning, ensuring the highest quality and taste for customers.

With over three decades of experience, Eagle Boys has perfected the art of pizza making while maintaining a commitment to freshness and quality that sets them apart in the competitive food industry.
